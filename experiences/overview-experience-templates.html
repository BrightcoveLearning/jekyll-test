Overview of Experience Templates

In this topic you will learn about the different experience templates that are available.

Experience templates control the overall look and feel of the experience. After you select a template, the template cannot be changed.

The following templates are available.

  • Corporate Communications

Each template provides a set of page templates that can be customized in the Experience Editor. The page templates can be viewed by editing an experience and then by clicking PAGES in the left navigation menu.

Click on a page name to open the page layout in the editor. In the above example, Home, Collections and Video Details pages are available.

Note: Some pages also provide the ability for the player layout to be changed. For more information, see Changing the Player Layout on a Portal Experience.

Corporate Communications

The Corporate Communications is used to create a native mobile application. When published, it can be deployed to iOS and Android devices.

The Mosaic template provides two page layout templates.

  • Home
  • Collections
  • Video Details
